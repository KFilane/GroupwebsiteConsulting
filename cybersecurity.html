What to know about Cyber Security


What is Cyber Security?


Cybersecurity refers to the collection of IT resources, practices, and guidelines that businesses use to protect their systems, networks, and data from unauthorized access and cyber threats. The primary goal of cybersecurity is to defend private and sensitive information from intrusions that typically attempt to obtain it for illegal purposes, financial extortion, or just to disrupt operations. The days of using simple-to-install antivirus software to safeguard your gadgets are long gone. Although the use of cloud services to store sensitive data has grown recently, there are still gaps in security regulations. Cybersecurity is now required, not an option.
