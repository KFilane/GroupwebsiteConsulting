What to know about Data Analysis


What is a data Analysis?


Data security encompasses the range of strategies, tools, and protocols that organizations employ to safeguard their data assets from unauthorized access and cyber threats. The main objective of data security is to protect sensitive information from breaches that could lead to data theft, financial losses, or disruption of operations. Gone are the days when basic antivirus software was sufficient to protect your devices. While the use of cloud services for data storage has become more prevalent, there are still gaps in security practices and regulations. Data security is now a necessity, not a choice, as organizations must ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of their data assets.
